Names of newborns registered in Berlin Charlottenburg-Wilmersdorf

Top names and newcomers

How man children were registered?

Registrations peaked in 2016.
Since then they show an unbroken downwards trend.

Which names are popular?

The favourite names have not changed much between 2012 and 2020. Most have, however, seen a decline in popularity - it seems like it is time for some newcomers!

Which names are up and coming?






In our list of newcomers we find some old classics: Karl, Frida and Mathilda are experiencing a revival. But there are also some real newbies like Liya or Nael.
